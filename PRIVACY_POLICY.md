## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you:

If you do not agree with this policy, please **do not install or use** the Game and uninstall it if already installed.

## 3. Information we process

### 3.1 Information stored on your device (local)

The Game stores gameplay and preference data **on your device** using Unity `PlayerPrefs` and similar local storage. This may include:

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Gameplay progress** | Campaign operation progress, unlock flags, best rounds cleared, commander XP, meta progression values | Save your progress between sessions |
| **Display name** | Optional nickname you enter (default may be “Legionnaire”) | Show on local leaderboard and UI |
| **Leaderboard entries** | Nickname, rounds cleared, rank title | Local “top scores” list on device |
| **Settings** | Master/music/SFX volume, battle speed | Remember your preferences |
| **Onboarding** | Tutorial completed, how-to-play seen, terms acceptance version | Avoid repeating prompts |
| **Legal consent** | Flag that you accepted Terms/Privacy (version number) | Demonstrate consent for store compliance |

This data **stays on your device** unless you back up your device through your OS, transfer devices, or we later add a cloud-save feature (we would update this policy first).

In short: gameplay data is stored locally on your device for game functionality. We do not run a direct player account system or direct in-game personal data collection pipeline in the current release.

**Legal basis (EEA/UK):** Performance of our contract with you (providing the Game) and our legitimate interest in operating a stable offline-capable experience.

### 3.2 Information we do not directly collect in the Game

In the **current release**, the Game does **not** include direct in-game collection channels for personal data (such as account registration forms or in-game support forms), and it does **not** intentionally collect:

- Precise GPS or continuous location  
- Contacts, photos, videos, or files  
- Microphone or camera input  
- Government ID, payment card numbers (payments are handled by the store)  
- Health or biometric data  
- Multiplayer voice or text chat (not offered)

### 3.3 Store purchase and platform data

When you **buy** the Game, **Google Play** or **Apple** processes payment. We receive limited information from the store (for example, that a purchase occurred, region, and fraud/refund signals) according to **their** policies—not your full card number.

This means platform providers may process certain transaction and technical data under their own policies, even when we do not run direct in-game personal data collection.

See:

- [Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) and [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
- [Apple Media Services Terms](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/) and [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### 3.4 Public store reviews

If you post a review or rating on Google Play or the App Store, that content is public and governed by the platform’s privacy policy. **Do not include personal information** (email, address, phone) in reviews.
